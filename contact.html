Frequently Asked Questions

You can sign up for upcoming cleanup events through our Events page. Simply browse the calendar, find an event that works for you, and click the "Sign Up" button. You'll receive a confirmation email with all the details you need.

We encourage community members to organize their own events. We can provide resources, guidance, and sometimes supplies to help make your event successful. Contact us through the form above or email volunteer@ecoclean.org to get started.

We offer various partnership opportunities for businesses, from sponsoring events to employee volunteer programs. Please contact us at info@ecoclean.org with "Business Partnership" in the subject line, and our partnerships coordinator will get back to you.

Yes, we have educational programs tailored for different age groups. We can arrange for guest speakers, provide lesson plans, or organize field trips. Visit our Resources page for more information or contact us directly to discuss your specific needs.

We appreciate your support! You can make a donation through our website's donation page, or contact us for information about in-kind donations, equipment sponsorships, or other ways to contribute.
