About Our Campaign

Our Mission

The Environmental Awareness and Cleanup Campaign is dedicated to creating a cleaner, healthier environment through community action and education. We believe that by working together, we can make a significant impact on reducing pollution and promoting sustainable practices.

Our mission is to empower individuals and communities to take responsibility for their local environment through organized cleanup events, educational workshops, and advocacy for sustainable policies.

Our History

The campaign began in 2018 when a small group of concerned citizens noticed the increasing amount of litter in local parks and waterways. What started as a single cleanup event with just 15 volunteers has grown into a community-wide movement with thousands of participants.

Over the years, we've expanded our focus beyond cleanups to include educational programs, partnerships with local schools and businesses, and advocacy for environmental policies that reduce waste and pollution at the source.
